Hydrate — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Hydrate is designed to respect your privacy completely. The short version: the app collects nothing about you. This page explains what that means.

Information we collect

None. Everything you enter into Hydrate — your water logs, daily goal, and reminder settings — is stored only on your own device. It is never sent to us or to anyone else.

No accounts, servers, analytics, or ads

Hydrate has no user accounts and makes no network requests. We operate no servers, use no analytics or tracking tools, and show no advertising. Because the app never transmits data, we never receive any information about you or how you use it.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, they are scheduled entirely on your device. No information related to notifications leaves your device.

Third parties

We do not sell, share, or disclose your information, because we never collect it in the first place. The app contains no third-party components that gather data.

Your data and how to delete it

Since your data lives only on your device, you are always in control of it. Deleting the app removes all of its data from your device.

Children's privacy

Hydrate does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
